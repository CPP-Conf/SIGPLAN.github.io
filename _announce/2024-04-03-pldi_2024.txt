PLDI is a premier forum for programming language research, broadly construed,
including design, implementation, theory, applications, and performance. Come
join us, online or in Copenhagen, Denmark, to discuss the latest programming
languages research!

* Website : https://pldi24.sigplan.org/attending/registration
* Time    : Mon 24--Fri 28 June 2024
* Place   : Radisson Blu Scandinavia, Amager Boulevard 70, 2300 Copenhagen S,
  Denmark

### Important dates

* Early Registration: 15th of May, 2024
