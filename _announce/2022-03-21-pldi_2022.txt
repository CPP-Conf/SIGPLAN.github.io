Call for Participation for PLDI 2022, the ACM SIGPLAN Conference on Programming Language Design and Implementation!
------------------------------------------------------------------------

PLDI is a premier forum for programming language research, broadly construed, including design, implementation, theory, applications, and performance. 
Come join us, online or in San Diego, to discuss the latest programming languages research!

* Website : https://pldi22.sigplan.org/attending/Registration
* Time    : 13th--17th June 2022
* Place   : Catamaran Resort in San Diego, California, USA + online option, comprising live streamed talks + Q&A and social interaction and events on “gather.town”.

### Important dates:
* Early Registration: On or before 16 May, 2022
