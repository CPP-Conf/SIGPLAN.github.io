---
title: "SIGPLAN Software Award Extension of Nomination Period"
timestamp: "2/6/2023 21:50:10"
---
The ACM SIGPLAN Executive Committee has approved extending this year's deadline for the SIGPLAN Software Award until Friday 3 March 2023. Please consider nominating strong candidates for the Software Award. Nominations remain eligible for three years. Details at [https://awards.sigplan.org](https://awards.sigplan.org).
